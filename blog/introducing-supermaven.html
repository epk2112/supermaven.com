FAQ

  • What types of fitness training do you offer?

    We offer a wide range of options, including strength training, cardio workouts, HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training), yoga, Pilates, and more. Our certified trainers will customize the training program based on your goals and preferences.

  • How long are the training sessions?

    Training sessions can vary in length depending on your needs and preferences. Sessions typically range from 30 minutes to 1 hour. Our trainers will work with you to determine the optimal session length for your goals and schedule.

  • Do you offer virtual training sessions?

    Yes, we offer virtual training sessions for individuals who prefer to train from the comfort of their own homes or have limited access to in-person sessions. Our trainers will guide you through the workouts via video call.

  • What equipment do I need for the training sessions?

    The equipment needed for the training sessions will depend on the specific workout plan. In some cases, bodyweight exercises may be sufficient, while in others, basic fitness equipment, such as dumbbells or resistance bands, may be required. Our trainers will provide guidance on the equipment needed for your training sessions.

  • What is your approach to nutrition and meal planning?

    Our trainers offer personalized nutritional guidance and meal planning as part of our fitness training services. They will assess your dietary needs and goals and create a customized nutrition plan that supports your fitness journey. We focus on balanced and sustainable nutrition to help you achieve long-term success.
